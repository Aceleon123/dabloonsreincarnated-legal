What is DabloonsReincarnated?

DabloonsReincarnated is a Discord bot that brings back the nostalgic "dabloons" trend with a new life, dabloons are now ready to use almost everywhere on Discord! Unlike real money systems, this economy is completely virtual and for fun only.

Key Features

  • 💸 Track your dabloon balance
  • 🎁 Give dabloons to other users
  • 📅 See when you joined the system
  • 🛒 Create your own little shop (WIP)

Privacy & Data

This bot only stores essential data for it to work, such as your Discord ID, the date you started using it, and your dabloon-related stats. Nothing more. Nothing gets sold, shared, or exploited.

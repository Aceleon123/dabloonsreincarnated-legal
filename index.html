What is Dabloons Reincarnated?

DabloonsReincarnated is a Discord bot that brings back the nostalgic "dabloons" trend with a new life. Your dabloons can now be tracked and shared across Discord, purely for fun and community engagement.

Key Features

  • 💸 Track your dabloon balance
  • 🎁 Give dabloons to other users
  • 📅 See when you joined the system
  • 🛒 Create your own little shop (WIP)

Privacy & Data

This bot only stores essential data: your Discord ID, the date you started using it, and your dabloon stats. Nothing more. Nothing gets sold, shared, or exploited.
